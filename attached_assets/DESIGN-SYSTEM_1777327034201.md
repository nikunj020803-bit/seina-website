## 2. Typography

The site uses **three** distinct type families. This contrast is the most defining stylistic choice.

### 2.1 Display + body — geometric humanist sans
- **Visual match:** Inter, Söhne, or Aeonik. The free Google-Fonts substitute is **Inter** (very close); a slightly tighter alternate is **Geist** or **Space Grotesk**.
- **Weights used:** 400 (regular), 500 (medium), 600 (semibold for inline emphasis like "30% more leads")
- **Letter-spacing on display:** `-0.02em` (tightened slightly)
- **Line-height:** `1.05–1.1` on H1/H2, `1.5` on body

### 2.2 Section labels + UI tags — monospace
- **Visual match:** Berkeley Mono or IBM Plex Mono. Free substitute: **JetBrains Mono** or **IBM Plex Mono**.
- **Used for:** "THE PROBLEM", "SOLUTION", "PLATFORM", "LEAD OPERATIONS CENTER", "BACKED BY Y", nav links ("PRODUCT", "CUSTOMERS"), button labels ("GET STARTED"), in-illustration tags ("FORM", "WORKFLOW", "TUE", "WED").
- **Always uppercase**, letter-spacing `+0.05em` to `+0.1em`, weight 500.

### 2.3 Pixel/8-bit numbers — only inside illustrations
- **Visual match:** A 5×7 bitmap face. Free substitutes: **VT323**, **Pixelify Sans**, or **Press Start 2P**.
- **Used for:** large numerals like "50", "96" inside the diagrams, plus the small "INBOUNDS" label beneath.

### 2.4 Type scale (px, desktop)
| Role | Size | Weight | Line height |
|---|---|---|---|
| Display H1 (hero) | 72 | 400 | 1.05 |
| Section H2 | 56 | 400 | 1.1 |
| Subsection H3 | 28 | 500 | 1.25 |
| Body large | 20 | 400 | 1.5 |
| Body | 17 | 400 | 1.55 |
| Mono label | 13 | 500 | 1 |
| Nav link | 13 | 500 | 1 |
| Pixel number (in diagrams) | 56 | 400 | 1 |

Mobile scale: H1 → 40, H2 → 32, body stays 17.

## 3. Layout & Spacing

- **Max content width:** 1240px, centered, 24–32px side padding.
- **Vertical section padding:** `120px` top/bottom on desktop, `80px` on tablet, `64px` on mobile.
- **Card grid:** 3 columns @ desktop, gap `24px`. Collapses to 1-col @ <900px.
- **Nav height:** 72px, sticky, white background, 1px bottom border `--line`.
- **Border-radius scale:** `0` for hard pixel-art tags, `4px` for buttons/cards in illustrations, `8px` for content cards, `999px` for the floating chat bubble only.
- **Standard space units:** 4 / 8 / 12 / 16 / 24 / 32 / 48 / 64 / 96 / 120.

## 4. Components

### 4.1 Tags / Pills (the small mono-cap badges)
- Outer: 1px solid border, 6–8px vertical / 12px horizontal padding.
- Variants:
  - **Neutral solid:** light gray fill (`#EFEFEF`), dark text. ("LEAD OPERATIONS CENTER")
  - **Dashed accent:** dashed orange border, light peach fill. ("BACKED BY Y" — with the Y in an orange square at the end)
  - **Cyan soft:** cyan-soft fill, dark text. ("WE'RE HIRING!")
  - **Section label:** transparent, mono-cap, paired with a small left-aligned icon (a pin/marker icon).

### 4.2 Buttons
- **Primary CTA:** `--surface-dark` fill, white mono-cap text, rounded 4px, 14px vertical / 24px horizontal padding, trailing arrow `→` separated by ~16px. Hover: subtle lift (translateY -1px) + shadow.
- **Secondary CTA:** `--surface-100` fill, dark mono-cap text, same dimensions.

### 4.3 Section header pattern
Every major section starts with the same triplet:
1. A small mono-cap label (`THE PROBLEM`, `SOLUTION`, `PLATFORM`, `CAPTURE`) prefixed by a 16px icon.
2. A 2-line large headline (H2, 56px, 400 weight, tight leading).
3. Either a 3-column card grid below, or a 2-column body+illustration layout.

### 4.4 Blueprint illustration card
This is the signature element. Recipe:
- Card background: dark `#141414` or light `#F2F2F2`.
- Subtle 32px-square grid overlay (1px lines @ 4–6% opacity).
- Pixel-style tags floating at structural points (LEADS in yellow, FORM in peach, etc.) with hard borders.
- Connector lines: 1.5px, slightly off-axis or hand-drawn feel, sometimes orange/curved.
- Four small "screw" dots in card corners (4–6px circles, slightly inset, gives a hardware-panel feel).
- Pixel-font numerals for any large stats.

### 4.5 Footer
- Background: `--surface-cyan`.
- 5-column grid: brand block (logo + tagline + copyright) + 4 link columns (PRODUCTS / LEARN MORE / SOCIALS / LEGAL).
- Column headings in mono-cap.
- **Ghost wordmark:** the brand name repeated at ~280px font-size, white at ~8% opacity, clipped at the bottom edge of the footer — fills the bottom band.

### 4.6 Floating chat bubble
- Fixed bottom-right, 56px circle, `--brand-blue` fill, white initial inside, mild drop shadow. Persists on every section.

## 5. Motion & Interaction

These weren't directly visible from screenshots but match the genre/style:

- **Scroll-reveal:** sections fade up 16px and fade in over 600ms, eased `cubic-bezier(0.2, 0.7, 0.2, 1)`. Triggered when ~20% of the section enters the viewport.
- **Diagram aliveness:** dots travel along connector lines on a 2–3s loop; stat numerals (50, 96, 30%) count up once on enter.
- **Button hover:** `transform: translateY(-1px)` + shadow `0 6px 16px rgba(0,0,0,0.12)`, 150ms ease.
- **Card hover:** very subtle border-color shift to `#111` and 1px lift.
- **Nav:** sticky; on scroll past 80px, gains a 1px bottom border + light shadow.
- **Marquee/ghost wordmark:** static (no scroll), but its low opacity makes it feel like a watermark.

## 6. Page Flow (top → bottom)

1. **Sticky nav** — logo, nav links (mono-cap), "WE'RE HIRING!" cyan pill, login link, dark "BOOK A DEMO" button.
2. **Hero** — two centered badges, large 2-line headline, subhead, two centered CTAs.
3. **The Problem** (dark section) — section label, 2-line headline, 3 dark blueprint cards each with title + description below.
4. **Platform** (light) — label, headline, body paragraph + primary CTA on left; colorful flowchart-of-icons illustration on right.
5. **Capture** (cyan band) — illustration left (tabbed workflow card), label + headline + 30% pill + 3 feature rows on right.
6. **Solution** (light) — label, 2-line headline, 3 numbered light blueprint cards (1, 2, 3) with title + description below.
7. **Bottom CTA** (light w/ diamond pattern) — large statement quote in a bordered box, single dark CTA.
8. **Footer** (cyan) — 5-column grid, ghost wordmark, copyright.
9. **Floating chat bubble** — persistent overlay.

---

## 7. Background patterns

- **Diamond pattern** (used behind the "B2B teams" CTA section): small rotated squares (~12px), light gray (`#E5E5E5`) at low opacity, repeated on a 32px grid.
- **Blueprint grid** (inside dark cards): 32px square grid, lines at 4% white.
- **Light blueprint** (inside cyan cards): 24px square grid, lines at 6% black.

---

## 8. Voice/copy patterns (so the structure feels right)

- Section labels are 1–2 words, ALL CAPS mono. Punchy: `THE PROBLEM`, `SOLUTION`, `PLATFORM`, `CAPTURE`.
- Headlines are 2 sentences run together, second one is the twist or payoff. ("Marketing did its job. The leads showed up. Then everything broke.")
- Subheads are factual, often with a number bolded.
- CTAs are 2-word imperatives in mono-cap with arrow: `GET STARTED →`, `BOOK A DEMO`, `SEE IT IN ACTION →`.

---

That's the whole system. The accompanying `index.html` is a single-file template wired to these tokens — drop your own copy and illustrations in, the look stays consistent.
